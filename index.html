Whispering Tree Grove

A floating tree responds to every whisper of your cursor. Move around the grove to sway the branches, spark the glow, and send the clouds drifting in new directions.

How it works

Hover anywhere in the grove to shift the focus of the light. As you near the canopy, the leaves glow brighter and the fruit shimmer. Glide past the roots to calm the tree and let the lights fade to twilight.

Everything is drawn with HTML and CSS, while a sprinkle of JavaScript keeps track of your pointer position. The custom properties that power the animation also adapt to touch events, so you can explore the scene on any device.

The story

This enchanted tree bends gently toward the light, following every movement. Hover near its branches to see the leaves twirl, or linger at the roots to watch the glow settle into twilight.

Crafted with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the illustration reacts in real time to your pointer, blending gradients and subtle transforms to create a serene, living scene.
